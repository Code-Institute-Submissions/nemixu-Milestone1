Meet our team!

Emma Grace

Emma is the Founder and CEO of Fibromyalgia Ireland. After her diagnosis of Fibromyalgia in 2009, Emma spent years researching the condition and began studying Social Studies to better learn how to help others with the condition. She founded Fibromyalgia Ireland in 2015.

Audrey O'Neill

Audrey is the co-founder of Fibromyalgia Ireland, committed to raising awareness of combined self care where alternative therapies and treatments can be combined to replace pharmaceuticals. Audrey is a lifecoach and clinical hypnosis practitioner who works building confidence and self esteem with young models and families.

John Lindsay

Retired Hospital CEO. After retirement in 2009 John joined the board of Chronic Pain Ireland and stepped down after nine years. He holds extensive knowledge of the health sector and in May 2019 joined the board of Fibromyalgia Irlenad clg and was elected Honorary Treasurer.

William McLoughlin

William McLoughlin BL, former secretary of Chronic Pain Ireland and board member of One-in-Four, is a barrister specializing in personal injuries litigation involving chronic pain and fibromyalgia, employment law and data protection law. William lectures at Independent Colleges Dublin and Griffith College.
